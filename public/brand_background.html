Our Legacy

Bespoke Tailoring is an offspring of the House of Parmar, founded in 1956 in Bur Dubai, Dubai, United Arab Emirates, by the late great master tailor from India, Mr. Pitamber Parmar, who went to Dubai in 1956 filled with hopes, dreams, and a deep knowledge of tailoring. Together with his three sons, he built a legacy of luxury tailoring in Dubai.

The first shop is Parmar Tailors, a custom-made-to-measure tailoring shop with locations in Bur Dubai and Jumeirah Lake Towers.

The Vision

Mr. Parmar trained his youngest son, Prakash Parmar, a 7th-generation tailor; he has been in the tailoring trade since he was 18 years old and felt that the family business can be managed well if one applies the principles of Bespoke tailoring to it.
As well as he was also disappointed with how “bespoke” is done in Dubai. Thus, the brand “Bespoke Tailoring” was founded, and he was determined to correct how bespoke tailoring is done in Dubai.

Prakash, a tailor and a businessman, specializes in bespoke tailoring management solutions, image consulting, and supplier management and relations. He is also one of the Directors of Master Tailors (WFMT). Prakash’s mission and advocacy is to introduce and provide “real” and genuine bespoke tailoring in Dubai.
Prakash has personally trained the tailors and staff at Bespoke and is very involved in the Bespoke Shop in The Gate DIFC, applying his knowledge, experience, skills, and touch to the shop’s operations.
